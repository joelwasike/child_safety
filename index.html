Safety Standards & Child Protection Policy

Our Commitment to Child Safety

We are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. Our app strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We take strong measures to prevent, detect, and report any violations of this policy.

Zero Tolerance Policy

We enforce a zero-tolerance policy toward:

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Sexual solicitation or grooming of minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Any behavior that exploits or endangers children

Prevention & Enforcement Measures

  • User reporting and blocking tools
  • Content moderation and review processes
  • Immediate account suspension for violations
  • Cooperation with law enforcement where required by law

User Reporting

Users are encouraged to report any suspicious or inappropriate behavior directly within the app. All reports are reviewed promptly and handled with seriousness and confidentiality.

Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable child protection laws and regulations and will report confirmed CSAE-related content to the appropriate authorities.

Contact Information

If you have concerns related to child safety or abuse prevention, please contact us:

  • Email: support@swapuzi.com
  • Support: support@swapuzi.com

We take all reports seriously and aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
